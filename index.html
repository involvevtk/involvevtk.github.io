Who We Are

InVoLveCultural and Charitable Society is a forum of enthusiastic and likeminded people who have joined together with a vision to create a forum for supporting various social, cultural and educational activities.

Our Vision

The vision of the InVoLveCultural and Charitable Society is to pave the strong foundation of overall social development initiatives to promise the social justice to all. We make sure the development and enhancement of such noble social causes through capacity building, developing mechanism and service delivery.

Our Story

It started with a group of nostalgic companions who wanted to have a feel of socially ‘involved’ for the good cause and welfare of society. Starting in 2016 January 26th, the initial group was subsequently joined by a limited number of friends from various backgrounds, but with a common interest in social cause and welfare activities. InVoLveCultural and Charitable Society is registered under societies registration act XXI of 1860.
